Welcome






Welcome to our website!
We are working towards building an exciting solution that allows
you to interact with the website based on your current emotions.
Our project is aimed towards delivering a wide variety of content to rehabilitation
centers and other facilieties that need to moniter their patients emotions closely.
The website allows the used to watch random "happy" videos,
guide through meditation exercises when scared/anxious,
alerts pre-determined members (hospital and family) while sharing
their location if they are angry or unresponsive.

Features like these are designed so that the medical professional or the family can still be alerted when they are not immedietly around the patient.
